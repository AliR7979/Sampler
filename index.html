Full York Ramen

The word "ramen" may have come from the Chinese word "Lamian", a type of Chinese Noodle.

The Shin-Yokohama Ramen Museum is the world's first food-themed amusement park.

Ingredient

  • Cooked Pork
  • Ramen Noodles
  • Chicken or Beef broth
  • Water
  • Carrots, Shredded
  • Thumb Sized Ginger
  • Scalliion or Chives
  • Shiitake mushrooms, Dried
  • Garlic
  • Naruto-Maki
  • Nori
  • Soy Sauce
  • Worcestershine Sauce
  • Chilli Oil
  • Sesame Oil
  • Soft-Boiled Eggs

Let's start Sluping!

  1. Get your filthiest Wok over the fire the best you can situate it
  2. Add Oil, over medium-low heat. Throw the garlic and ginger; stir fry for 2 minutes or until soft and fragrant.
  3. Add the broth and the water. Bring to a simmer; add the mushrooms and simmer for 10 minutes
  4. Add the instant noodles until the noodles have softened. Add the scallions and stir to combine.
  5. Remove from heat, stir in the carrots,and top with a soft-boiled egg. Season with chili oil, hot sauce, sesame oil, and/or soy sauce and salt to taste.
  6. Serve with Nori, Naruto-Maki and a giant spoon
